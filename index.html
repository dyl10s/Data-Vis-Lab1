Defining a Goal and Data Set

The data set I will be using is from the FDIC (Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation). I will be looking at all the failed banks from October 13th, 2000 to November 1st, 2019. With this data I will be looking to see if there are major outlier states that have a lot of bank failures as well as seeing if the location of the state effects how many banks fail.
