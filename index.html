Step 1: Defining a Goal and Data Set

The data set I will be using is from the FDIC (Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation). I will be looking at all of the failed banks from October 13th, 2000 to November 1st 2019. With this data I will be looking to see if there is a relationship between the time of year and when a bank fails. On top of this I will also create a graph displaying which states have the highest failing rate and see if the location of the bank has anything to do with the failure rate.
